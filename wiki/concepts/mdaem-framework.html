MDAEM v3.4 (Multi-Dimensional Asset Evaluation Matrix)

Definition: A gated, weighted, scenario-driven decision system for evaluating individual securities against a MAX-RETURN / asymmetric-risk-reward mandate, calibrated to Seeking Alpha Quant ratings and iterated empirically every 3 months. Authored by Aphiwat (Thai retail investor, Dime! app) over multiple versions (mdaem-v34-framework).

Scope note (important): This page documents Aphiwat's own decision tool, which explicitly produces sizing and buy/sell verdicts. It is not a behavior this vault adopts — the Futurist Investor engine remains insight-only (CLAUDE.md §10). See mdaem-vs-futurist-investor.

The pipeline (decision flow)

TICKER → Triage → Data pull → SA Quant pull
   → 10 Hard Gates → Score 10 Dimensions
   → MDAEM Total → Tier
   → SA Overlay Cap → Refinements
   → Scenario Math → Sizing
   → Entry Plan → Validation Tracking

1. Triage (60 seconds)

Reject immediately unless: real company (not penny <$1), in universe (US/HK/China/Thai/VN), adequate liquidity (avg daily volume × price > $10M), and the surface story warrants investigation.

2. The 10 Hard Gates (ALL must pass — any fail → REJECT)

# Gate Pass criterion
1 Asymmetry Dim 10 ≥5 (bull/bear return ratio ≥1.5:1)
2 Catalyst Specific value-unlock <36 months
3 Liquidity 5-day exit at <20% price impact
4 Accounting No active SEC/restatement issues
5 Bear case Articulable in 2–3 sentences
6 SA Quant Not Sell or Strong Sell
7 Revisions EPS up/down ratio ≥0.2 (Ref L)
8 M&A ceiling Not within ±5% of acquisition price (Ref 17)
9 Multiple P/S and EV/Sales <50x sector (Ref 20)
10 Financing Cash runway past catalyst (Ref 21)

3. The 10 Weighted Dimensions (score 1–10 × weight → /100)

Dim Weight Question
1 Quality/Moat 15% Durable competitive advantage?
2 Financial Health 10% Cash flow, debt, runway?
3 Valuation 15% Cheap vs sector & history?
4 Earnings Quality 10% FCF tracks earnings?
5 Growth 10% Revenue + margin trajectory?
6 Technical 10% Trend and momentum?
7 Sentiment 10% Crowded or contrarian?
8 Catalysts 10% Specific events <36mo?

5. Seeking Alpha Quant overlay (caps sizing)

The MDAEM tier is cross-checked against the seeking-alpha-quant rating; the overlay caps sizing (e.g. T1 + SA Hold → cap 8%; any tier + SA Sell → REJECT). Rule: when MDAEM and SA conflict, take the LOWER signal.

6. Scenario math (mandatory before sizing)

Explicit Bull/Base/Bear with probabilities → Expected Value = Σ(prob × return); and Asymmetry ratio = |bull return| / |bear return| (must be ≥1.5:1 or the asymmetry gate fails). "If you can't state your variant view vs consensus, you don't have an edge — skip the trade."

7. Sizing, modifiers, entry plan

Pre-modifier sizing from the overlay table, then modifiers (52-wk-low +fundamentals +1%; +500% 12mo −1–2%; sector >25% −1–2%; VIX>25 −1–2%; binary spec cap 2%). Then entry/stops/time-stops and a position category (Foundation 15–25% · scarcity hedge 8–12% · core compounder 8% · conviction satellite 5–7% · STARTER 3–4% · speculative 1–2% · cash 3–10%).

9. The validation loop (the engine's "nervous system")

Every decision logs measurable 3-month success/failure criteria into a validation database; quarterly review scores predictions, calculates alpha vs S&P, and proposes new refinements. The live record: mdaem-validation-database.
