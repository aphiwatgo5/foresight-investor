Overview

The current state of knowledge in this wiki — your starting point. It orients you to what the wiki
is about and where the synthesis currently stands. Kept current as sources are ingested and scans
run. For the fast rolling summary, read hotcache.md first.

What this wiki is

A persistent, LLM-maintained second brain in an Obsidian vault. Sources go into raw/ (immutable);
the LLM reads them and compiles their knowledge into interlinked pages under wiki/. The wiki is a
compounding artifact — cross-references, contradictions, and synthesis are built once and kept
current, not re-derived on every question (wiki-as-compounding-artifact). Rules live in
CLAUDE.md; workflow mode is discuss-first.

How it operates

Core operations — ingest-query-lint:
- Ingest: add a source → summarize, file, cross-link, log.
- Query: ask a question → answer from the wiki with citations; good answers get filed as syntheses.
- Lint: health-check for contradictions, orphans, stale claims, gaps.
- Scan / Bubble-watch (investing vertical, below): periodic radar updates + on-demand bubble-risk reads.

Two domains of knowledge

1. Knowledge systems & multi-agent simulation (the founding theme — LLMs doing labor humans
won't). The founding pattern (the-llm-wiki-pattern, ancestor memex / vannevar-bush) uses
LLMs to compile and maintain knowledge; mirofish uses many LLM agents to simulate worlds and
predict outcomes (usage: mirofish-how-to-use-and-applications). Both impose structure over raw
inputs — synthesis (wiki-as-compounding-artifact) vs. simulation (multi-agent-simulation /
swarm-intelligence / graphrag).

2. The "Futurist Investor" engine (CLAUDE.md §10) — the wiki's dominant active vertical:
long-term thematic investment research, insight-only, never buy/sell advice. Structured as
7 meta-forces → 28 theme pages (analyst-grade template with comparative player tables), governed
by three living hubs: trend-radar (conviction/stage table), watchlist (triggers +
invalidations), prediction-ledger (dated falsifiable calls). Cross-theme dependencies in
theme-linkage-map; macro overlay in macro-regime. A complementary bottom-up single-name
system, mdaem-framework (with seeking-alpha-quant calibration), is documented as the
operator's own tool — see mdaem-vs-futurist-investor.
