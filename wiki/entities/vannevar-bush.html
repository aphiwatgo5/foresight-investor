Vannevar Bush

What it is: The thinker who proposed the memex in 1945 — the conceptual ancestor of
the LLM wiki pattern.

Summary

Bush envisioned a personal, curated knowledge store with associative trails between
documents — private, actively curated, where the connections between documents are as
valuable as the documents themselves. The LLM wiki pattern is explicitly framed as a
realization of this vision (the-llm-wiki-pattern).

Key facts

  • Proposed the memex in 1945. (the-llm-wiki-pattern)
  • His vision was closer to a private curated store than to what the web became. (the-llm-wiki-pattern)
  • The unsolved part of his vision was who does the maintenance — the LLM solves this. (the-llm-wiki-pattern)

Relationships

  • Originator of memex, which prefigures wiki-as-compounding-artifact.
