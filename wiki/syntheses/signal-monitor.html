Signal Monitor

Bottom line: The engine's rolling sensor panel — the early-warning layer that catches regime
shifts between scans. One row per metric the engine watches; each carries a warn level (attention)
and a flip level (thesis-changing). Updated every weekly MONITOR (CADENCE.md) from

How to read. Status: 🟢 calm · 🟡 warn (watch) · 🔴 flip (act — check decision-register /
regime-monitor). The flip column is the level that changes a thesis; the warn column is
the level that prompts a closer look. Every reading is live-pulled with provenance — no training-data values.
