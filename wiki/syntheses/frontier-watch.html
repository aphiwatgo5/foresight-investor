Frontier Watch

Bottom line: The foresight/trend-capture skill — a disciplined way to detect emerging themes
before consensus, the way AI / bitcoin / clean energy once were. Each candidate is scored on four
axes; only those crossing the threshold earn a wiki/concepts/ page. This is where the next
conviction-4 theme comes from. For the operator's own book; not advice.

The discipline. The goal is not "what's hyped" but "what is becoming investable." A theme
earns promotion when adoption-curve stage, hype-vs-substance balance, investable purity, and
time-to-liquidity all clear. Hype alone (quantum 2023, metaverse 2021) fails this filter.

The four-axis scoring framework

Axis Question 1 (avoid) 3 (watch) 5 (promote)
Adoption curve Where on the S-curve? Pre-working prototype Pilots / early revenue Inflecting to mass adoption
Hype vs substance Is the tech real now? Pure narrative Real but unproven at scale Proven unit economics / cash flows
Investable purity Is there a clean way in? Private only (SpaceX) Pure-plays but tiny/illiquid Liquid pure-plays or clean ETFs
Time-to-liquid When liquid, fairly-priced exposure? >5y 2–5y <2y

Promotion threshold: avg ≥3.5 AND no axis =1. Below → watchlist. The existing radar themes all
scored ≥3.5 at their 2026-06-01 inclusion.

The trend-capture methodology (how to add candidates)

  1. Source broadly: quarterly, sweep a horizon-scanning list (CB Insights / a16z / MIT TR /
    Bloomberg megatrends / your own reading) for themes not yet on the radar.
  2. Score on the 4 axes — be honest about hype vs substance; the engine's own backtest shows
    hype-stage names get punished.
  3. Check investability — can you actually buy it cleanly? SpaceX = no; that caps the score.
  4. Track the sensors — each candidate carries 1–2 falsifiable conditions (like the radar's
    invalidations) that, when met, lift the score.
  5. Promote at avg ≥3.5 → create wiki/concepts/<slug>.md (analyst-grade template), add to
    trend-radar under the right meta-force, file any new instruments in watchlist.
