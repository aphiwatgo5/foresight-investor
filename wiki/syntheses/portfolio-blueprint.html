Portfolio Blueprint

Bottom line: How the engine's decisions assemble into a whole — correlation-aware allocation,
not just a theme list. The central insight from theme-linkage-map: diversified across themes
can still be one bet if everything hangs off the AI-capex demand engine. This page sets the
target allocation per meta-force, the position-category buckets, and the concentration caps that
prevent single-cluster blowup. For the operator's own book; not advice to others.

Scope. Allocation targets at the meta-force / theme level. Specific tickers + sizing live in
decision-register; the MDAEM methodology in mdaem-framework. This page is the construction
layer between them.

The barbell (the core construction idea)

The bubble read (bubble-risk-us-equity-2026-06-28) and bubble-implications-by-theme point to
one stance: own the reflexive AI engine, but cap it and hedge with genuine diversifiers.

Side Purpose Themes Target
Offensive core Capture the dominant force (AI capex +60–77% YoY, P1 ✓) semiconductors · ai-infrastructure · power-grid-electrification · cybersecurity · networking-optical 35–45%
Defensive diversifiers Low-correlation compounders that hold up in an AI de-rate defense-security · glp1-obesity-metabolic · japan-corporate-reform · water-resource-scarcity · commodities-gold-real-assets 25–35%

The barbell is intentionally lopsided toward offense while the bubble's structural extreme has not
flipped to euphoria (F&G 43.6, not >75 — signal-monitor). As regime-monitor shifts acts, the
barbell rebalances: trim offense, raise diversifiers + cash.

Concentration caps (the hard rules)

These are non-negotiable — they are what survives the correlated de-rating the theme-linkage-map
warns about.

  • No single ticker >8% (TSM/LLY cap at 8% even at conv-4; STARTERs 3–4%).
  • No single theme >12% (cybersecurity basket caps at 5–6%; semis via SMH + TSM ≤12% combined).
  • AI-capex complex ≤25% total — this is the diversification-killer cluster. Even if every name
    is conv-4, the cluster is the bet. Hard ceiling.
  • Cash never <3% — dry powder discipline for the watchlist trigger adds.
  • HY OAS >3.5% → freeze new AI-cluster adds; >4.0% monthly close → trim offense by 5 points
    (P17/P21 regime logic, see regime-monitor).

Rebalance logic

  1. Drift rebalance — any meta-force >5 points off target → trim the overweight, add the under.
  2. Signal rebalance — a signal-monitor flip → act per regime-monitor (e.g., F&G>75 trim
    offense 5pts; HY>4% trim offense 5pts + raise cash).
  3. Decision-led rebalance — new ADD/EXIT in decision-register → reflects here automatically.
  4. Quarterly REVIEW (CADENCE.md) — full re-weight; check the correlation clusters still hold.
