Bubble-Framework Implications → Per-Theme Investment Stance

Bottom line: The bubble-risk-us-equity-2026-06-28 read — structurally extreme (CAPE 40.7,
Buffett 219%, margin debt +54% YoY, negative ERP, Mag-7 ≈33%) but sentiment fearful, not euphoric —
does not call a top. What it does is tell you where the fragility is concentrated and what
diversifies it. This page turns that into a research stance for each of the 30 themes.
Insight & research only — not buy/sell advice or position sizing.

What the bubble framework implies (the 5 takeaways)

  1. The fragility is one complex, not the whole market. Soros-reflexivity + concentration show the
    risk is the AI-capex loop (capex → expectations → price → cheap capital → more capex),
    expressed through the Mag-7. A de-rating there re-rates the correlated themes together
    (theme-linkage-map).
  2. Valuation caps conviction; it does not time exits. Shiller (ECY ≈ +0.4%) and Dalio gauge 1 say
    forward returns are structurally low — so entry discipline matters more than usual (hand off to
    mdaem-framework for single-name sizing). High trend conviction ≠ attractive entry.
  3. Credit is the early-warning gauge. Minsky: HY spreads are tight (~2.6%) and complacent. The
    first ≥400–450 bps move (P17) is the signal that the speculative phase is turning — watch it
    above any equity indicator.
  4. Sentiment is the missing ingredient. F&G 25 (Extreme Fear) is why this is a mania phase, not
    a climax. A flip to Extreme Greed would raise near-term top risk, not lower it.
  5. Therefore: barbell. Stay invested in the structural winners but tilt toward (a) non-
    discretionary AI-levered demand and physical bottlenecks with real contracts, and (b) genuine low-
    correlation diversifiers.     Avoid adding to the richest, most reflexive, most crowded expressions.

Exposure key: 🔴 Core = the AI-capex reflexive engine (correlated de-rating risk) · 🟠
Correlated = rides the same demand/capex · 🟡 Adjacent = partial overlap + own cycle · 🟢
Diversifier = low AI-capex correlation · ⚪ Idiosyncratic = own cycle (rate/crypto/EM/binary).

The barbell, concretely

  • Keep (structural winners, add on weakness): semiconductors (TSM), ai-infrastructure on drawdowns.
  • Tilt toward — defensive AI-levered: cybersecurity, power-grid-electrification, water-resource-scarcity.
  • Tilt toward — true diversifiers: defense-security, glp1-obesity-metabolic, japan-corporate-reform, commodities-gold-real-assets.
  • Trim/avoid adding: the richest, most reflexive expressions — momentum semis at peak, rich defense-tech (Palantir), ai-software-agents "show-me" names, crowded cyber at >20× sales.
