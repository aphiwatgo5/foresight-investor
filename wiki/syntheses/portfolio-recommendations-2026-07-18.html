What this is not

  • Not a forecast. The recommended book is a snapshot of where the methodology points today;
    it changes monthly as signals update.
  • Not all 29 names are buys. Each still needs the operator's manual MDAEM pass (Dim 1/2/4/8/10 +
    10 gates). The framework surfaces candidates; it does not replace judgment.
  • Not regime-adjusted in real-time. Today's book is computed off the current bull-regime tilt;
    if regime-monitor flips to Act-2, the macro-rotation sleeve auto-rotates defensive (per its
    rules) but the operator must manually trim momentum.
  • 2022-2024 backtest columns have a coverage artifact — flagged above. The 5y CAGR/Sharpe
    numbers are robust (computed from full daily returns); the per-year holdings snapshots are
    reliable from 2025 forward.
