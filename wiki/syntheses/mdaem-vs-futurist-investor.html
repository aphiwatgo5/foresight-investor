MDAEM vs the Futurist Investor engine — two complementary systems

Bottom line: Aphiwat runs two distinct investing systems that fit together like macro and micro lenses. The vault's Futurist Investor engine (CLAUDE.md §10) does top-down thematic research and is insight-only. MDAEM v3.4 does bottom-up single-name decisions and does produce sizing and buy/sell verdicts. They are complementary — but must stay operationally separate so the vault never drifts into personalized advice.

How they fit together

  1. Theme → name funnel. The vault identifies where to look (e.g. ai-infrastructure, nuclear-uranium, quantum-computing convictions); MDAEM decides whether and how much of a specific name to hold.
  2. Cross-check on shared names. Both touch the same tickers/themes — CCJ (uranium), MU/AMD (semis), QBTS/RGTI/IONQ (quantum), gold, space. The vault's thematic conviction and MDAEM's single-name verdict should be consistent; divergence is a useful flag.
  3. Two falsifiable ledgers. prediction-ledger (thematic, insight-only) and the MDAEM validation DB (single-name, sized) are parallel hit-rate trackers. They can be reconciled at review time without merging — e.g., MDAEM's H4 (SPCX below IPO) echoes the vault's space-economy caution.

The guardrail boundary (non-negotiable)

  • The vault documents MDAEM as Aphiwat's tool; it does not start issuing sized buy/sell calls itself.
  • MDAEM sizing tables, ADD/REJECT verdicts, and book weights live in the source/record pages (mdaem-framework, mdaem-validation-database) as description of the operator's framework, not as wiki recommendations.
  • When asked thematic questions, the vault answers in insight-only mode; when the operator wants a single-name sizing decision, that is MDAEM's job, run by Aphiwat.

Evidence for / against complementarity

  • For: Different units (theme vs name), different outputs (conviction vs size), shared universe → natural macro/micro division of labor. Both already exist and are actively maintained.
  • Tension: Overlapping coverage risks double-counting or contradictory signals on the same name; the fix is explicit cross-reference, not unification.

Confidence & gaps

  • High confidence on the structural complementarity (the two systems are designed for different jobs).
  • Gap: no formal reconciliation step yet exists between the prediction-ledger and the MDAEM validation DB; proposing one is a candidate for the next scan or the 2026-08-24 MDAEM review.
