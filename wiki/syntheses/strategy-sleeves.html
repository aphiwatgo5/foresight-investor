Strategy Sleeves — Master Table

Bottom line: The 4 backtested strategy sleeves that compose the v4.0 portfolio. Each is a
rules-based, quantitatively-scored allocation with measured CAGR/Sharpe/drawdown. The blend
allocates capital across styles so the book works in any regime (the backtest-results-2026-07-18
regime-split shows why this matters: raw momentum is +100%/yr in bull regimes but −46%/yr in bear).

How the sleeves plug into the portfolio

The 4 sleeves are an orthogonal layer to the existing 7-meta-force allocation in
portfolio-blueprint. A single name can sit in 2 sleeves (e.g., TSM is both Quality + Momentum).
The meta-force caps still apply (no ticker >8%, AI-capex cluster ≤25%). The sleeve weights are
the style allocation; the meta-force weights are the theme allocation — both must be respected.
