Track Record

Bottom line: The P&L and accountability layer for the engine's sized decisions — the answer to
"does this actually work?" Every ADD/EXIT in decision-register is scored here on close; hit-rate
and alpha are computed by conviction so the engine can calibrate itself. Extends the
mdaem-backtest-2026-06-28 discipline from the MDAEM names to the whole vault. For the operator's
own book; not advice.

Scope. Two parallel ledgers feed this page: (1) prediction-ledger — qualitative/falsifiable
theme-level calls (P1–P22); (2) decision-register — sized ticker-level decisions (D-series).
This page scores the D-series (realized + unrealized P&L) and rolls up both into a composite
calibration read. Updated whenever a decision closes or a P-call resolves.
